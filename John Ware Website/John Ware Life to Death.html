John Ware (c.1845-12th September 1905)

Ware was born into slavery in South Carolina but after the American Civil War he moved to Texas where he learned the skills of a rancher and became a cowboy.

He moved to what became Alberta.A bit after he arrived in Calgary he got his own ranch near Red Deer River.

He had 5 children with his wife Mildred Lewis.In 1905 there was a flood in what is now called Ware Creek that destroyed his home.

Three years after this incident Mildred died of Pneumonia and John died in a horse incident where his horse tripped over a badger hole which crushed John and snapped it's neck.
